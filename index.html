About

Mathurin Children's Foundation is a non-profit social organization created in December 2015 to invest in the lives of Senegalese (West Africa) children.

Our vision is to end the cycle of child poverty and ensure that every child is healthy, happy, secure and receives an education.

Our mission is to improve the quality of life of Senegalese children in need, reduce malnutrition, child abuse and neglect.
